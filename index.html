Our Story

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

For over 40 years, Anthony has served Henderson with honest, expert watch and jewelry repair. What began as a small family shop has become one of the valley's most trusted names in restoration.

We treat every timepiece as if it were our own—with precision, respect, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Whether it's a vintage family heirloom or a modern luxury watch, our expertise brings it back to life.
